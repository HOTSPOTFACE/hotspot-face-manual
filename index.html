1. Introduction

The Hotspot-Face is an advanced touchscreen monitor specifically designed for DMR hotspot systems operating PI-Star.

It gives you real-time visibility of your DMR activity without needing to sit at a PC. Callsigns, talk groups, connection status, and transmission duration are all displayed clearly on a dedicated screen in your shack.

Key benefits

  • Instant DMR activity display
  • No computer needed for monitoring
  • Touch-based configuration
  • Automatic network detection
  • Dual-mode WiFi / Hotspot operation

3. Initial Setup

On first boot, the device starts in its own WiFi setup mode.

  1. Power the device via USB-C.
  2. On your phone or PC, connect to the WiFi network: Hotspot-FACE Setup
    Password: M3URE1512
  3. Open a browser and go to: 192.168.4.1
  4. Enter your home WiFi SSID and password.
  5. Enter your PI-Star IP addresses (WiFi and Hotspot if you use both).
  6. Enable Auto-Discovery if unsure of your PI-Star IP.
  7. Save settings and allow the device to reboot.
Tip: If you do not know your PI-Star IP, turn on Auto-Discovery and let the Hotspot-Face locate it on your network.

4. Display Layout

Header Shows your device name. Tap to access settings.
Status Idle, TX, or Error state.
Callsign Current transmitting station.
Talk Group Active talk group or reflector.
Duration Length of current or last TX.
Date Current date and day.
Connection Mode (WiFi / Hotspot) and PI-Star IP.

Status indicators

  • Idle: No active transmission
  • TX: Active transmission (shown in red)
  • WiFi Off: No WiFi connection
  • No PI-Star: Monitor cannot reach PI-Star
  • Auth Error: Credentials for PI-Star are wrong
  • Connected successfully
  • Connection failed

5. Normal Operation

Automatic Operation

After configuration, the Hotspot-Face operates automatically:

  • Connects to WiFi on startup
  • Detects whether PI-Star is reachable via WiFi or Hotspot mode
  • Fetches live DMR activity every second
  • Updates callsign, talk group, status, and duration

Network Mode Switching

  • WiFi Mode ‚Äì When connected to your home network
  • Hotspot Mode ‚Äì When connected directly to PI-Star hotspot
